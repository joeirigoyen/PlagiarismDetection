Bon Jovi is an American rock band that was formed in New Jersey in 1983. The band is known for its catchy hooks, soaring guitar riffs, and anthemic choruses. Led by frontman Jon Bon Jovi, the band has released 14 studio albums and sold over 100 million records worldwide. Hits such as "Livin' on a Prayer," "Wanted Dead or Alive," and "It's My Life" have become staples of rock radio and continue to resonate with audiences around the world. With their high-energy performances and sing-along choruses, Bon Jovi has established themselves as one of the most successful and enduring rock bands of all time.
